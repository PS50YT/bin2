EaglerXServer


A Spigot, BungeeCord, and Velocity plugin that allows Eaglercraft 1.5.2, EaglercraftX 1.8, and Eaglercraft 1.12.2 players to join your Java Edition server.
Updating to EaglerXServer is highly recommended if you are currently using EaglerXBungee or EaglerXVelocity on your server.
